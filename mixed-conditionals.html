🔀 Mixed Conditionals

Aprende a combinar tiempos verbales para expresar situaciones donde la condición y el resultado ocurren en momentos diferentes (nivel B2).

📖 Teoría rápida — Mixed Conditionals

🔀 Tipo A: Pasado → Presente (3rd → 2nd)

If + Past Perfect , would + infinitive

Condición en el pasado que no se cumplió → resultado en el presente.

If I had studied medicine, I would be a doctor now.
(No estudié medicina en el pasado → no soy médico ahora)

🔀 Tipo B: Presente → Pasado (2nd → 3rd)

If + Past Simple , would have + Past Participle

Condición en el presente (irreal) → resultado en el pasado.

If I weren't afraid of flying, I would have visited Japan last year.
(Tengo miedo a volar ahora → no visité Japón en el pasado)

⚖️ Comparativa rápida

3rd (normal): If I had studied, I would have passed. (pasado → pasado)
Mixed A (3→2): If I had studied, I would be a doctor now. (pasado → presente)
Mixed B (2→3): If I were rich, I would have bought that house. (presente → pasado)
2nd (normal): If I were rich, I would buy that house. (presente → futuro)
[3→2] If she had taken that job, she would be rich now. 💰
[2→3] If he spoke Italian, he would have helped us with the translation. 🇮🇹
[3→2] We wouldn't be lost now if we had followed the map. 🗺️

✏️ Ejercicios — Mixed Conditionals

Completa cada frase con la forma verbal correcta. Hay dos tipos: 3→2 (pasado→presente) y 2→3 (presente→pasado).

1. If I ______ (not break) my leg, I would play football now.

2. She ______ (be) a doctor now if she had studied medicine.

3. If he ______ (not be) afraid of flying, he would have visited Japan last year.

4. If I ______ (take) that job, I would be rich now.

5. We ______ (not be) lost now if we had followed the map.

6. If she ______ (speak) Italian, she would have helped us with the translation.

7. If they ______ (save) more money, they would be able to buy a house now.

8. I ______ (not feel) tired today if I had gone to bed earlier.

9. If I ______ (have) his phone number, I would have called him yesterday.

10. If it ______ (not be) so cold today, we would have gone to the beach yesterday.
