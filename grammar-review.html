Grammar Review

Repaso rápido de la gramática básica. Ideal para calentar antes de temas más avanzados.

📖 Teoría rápida

⏰ Present Simple
Subject + verb(+s) + complements
Neg: don't / doesn't + verb (base form)
Int: Do / Does + subject + verb (base form)?
Uso: rutinas, verdades universales, horarios
Adverbios: always, usually, sometimes, rarely, never
🔄 Present Continuous
Subject + am/is/are + verb(-ing)
Neg: am/is/are + not + verb(-ing)
Uso: acciones en progreso ahora, planes futuros
Adverbios: now, right now, at the moment, today
Stative verbs: like, love, hate, know, believe, want (NO se usan en continuous)
📅 Past Simple
Subject + verb (past form)
Neg: didn't + verb (base form)
Int: Did + subject + verb (base form)?
Uso: acciones terminadas en el pasado
Adverbios: yesterday, last week, two days ago, in 2010
⏳ Past Continuous
Subject + was/were + verb(-ing)
Uso: acción en progreso en un momento del pasado
Adverbios: while, when, at that moment
🔮 Future: will vs going to
Will: decisiones espontáneas, predicciones, promesas
Subject + will + verb · Neg: won't + verb
Going to: planes ya decididos, predicciones con evidencia
Subject + am/is/are + going to + verb
