Vocabulary

Vocabulario de las Units 1, 3, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10 y 11 según las páginas de referencia del PDF oficial.

📖 Referencia por unidades

Basado en las páginas de referencia (fondo rosa) del PDF de vocabulario. Cada unidad tiene un círculo con términos clave, colocaciones y expresiones.

📘 Unit 1 — Personality adjectives · Collocations

🔵 Personality adjectives: serious, cheerful, brave, positive, talented, creative, active, laid‑back, charming
🔗 Collocations: make a mistake / an appearance · do your best / work · get a present / a reputation · lose a race / your life · take a risk / a photograph · form a group / an opinion
💬 JUST phrases: It was just a joke · Tom's just arrived · The book was just fantastic

📘 Unit 3 — Types of films/TV · Expressions with GET

🎬 Types of films: documentary, comedy, action film, animated film, horror, thriller, science fiction (sci‑fi), romantic comedy (rom‑com)
📺 Types of TV programmes: game show, chat show, sports programme, reality show, sitcom, cartoon, soap (opera), drama series, talent show
🔗 GET expressions: get angry, get bored, get a drink, get better, get there

📘 Unit 5 — Musical instruments · Making music · OUT

🎵 Musical instruments: trumpet, violin, guitar, piano, drums, saxophone, keyboard, bass guitar
🎤 Making music: download a song, write songs & lyrics, enter a talent show, go on tour, start a band, record / release a single, enter the charts, win a competition, play a gig
🔗 Phrasal verbs with OUT: start out, find out, run out of, come out, go out, sort out

📘 Unit 6 — The environment · Verbs to talk about energy

🌍 The environment: litter, water / air pollution, flood, recycling, melt, extinct, rubbish, waste, laws, smog
⚡ Verbs to talk about energy: to recycle, to throw away, to waste, to charge, to reuse, to leave on standby, to disconnect, to save

📘 Unit 7 — Future time · Arranging a party · ABOUT

⏰ Future time expressions: the day after tomorrow, the (week/month) after next, before too long, next (week/month/year/weekend), four (days/weeks) from now, later (today/this week), in the near future, in (two weeks' / a year's) time
🎉 Arranging a party: choose a theme, decorate the room, draw up a guest list, get permission, book/hire a DJ, send out invitations, organise food & drinks
💬 Phrases with ABOUT: about (10 o'clock / half an hour), about to (go home), What about you?, sorry about (that), forget about (it), think about (it)

📘 Unit 8 — Direction and movement · Science

🧭 Direction and movement: away from, around, down, backwards, towards, up and down, forwards, up
🔬 Science: laboratory, scientist, discovery, experiment, machine, invention, cure, invent, do research

📘 Unit 9 — Jobs · Time with IN · Work as/in/for · Work vs Job

💼 Jobs (pay & conditions): work long hours, earn money, get paid holidays, give in your notice, on‑the‑job training, start a career, a challenging job, be promoted, be successful
⏰ Time expressions WITH IN: in the end, in the next few years, in the past, in a day, in three hours, in June, in two weeks' time
🔗 Work as / in / for: work as a teacher, work in television, work for a big bank
⚖️ Work vs Job: a good job (countable, puesto) · difficult work (uncountable, concepto)

📘 Unit 10 — Time linkers · Illness collocations

⏱️ Time linkers: when, then, until
🤒 Illness collocations: take exercise, get better, see a doctor, have an operation, make an appointment, feel sick

📘 Unit 11 — Fun · Verbs + object + infinitive · MAKE

🎉 Fun & leisure: have fun, enjoy oneself, play games, go out with friends, watch a film, listen to music, spend time, relax
🔗 Verbs with object + infinitive: want someone to do, ask someone to do, tell someone to do, expect someone to do, would like someone to do
🔗 Expressions with MAKE: make plans, make a decision, make a phone call, make a mistake, make someone laugh, make noise, make the bed, make friends, make sure
