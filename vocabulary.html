Vocabulary

Vocabulario de las Units 1, 3, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10 y 11 del libro de texto.

📖 Referencia por unidades

Estos son los bloques de vocabulario que entran en el examen. Usa esta guía para estudiar — cada unidad tiene sus temas específicos.

📘 Unit 1 — Personality adjectives · Collocations

Adjetivos de personalidad: generous, lazy, honest, kind, helpful, friendly, outgoing, sociable, caring, considerate, thoughtful, impatient, temperamental, brave, shy, funny…
Collocations con have / be / look / feel

📘 Unit 3 — Expressions with GET

GET: get up (levantarse), get on (subir / llevarse bien), get along (llevarse bien), get out (salir/bajarse), get off (bajarse), get in (entrar), get to (llegar a), get used to (acostumbrarse), get dressed (vestirse), get bored (aburrirse), get lost (perderse), get ready (prepararse)

📘 Unit 5 — Making music + instruments · Expressions with OUT

Música e instrumentos: guitar, piano, drums, violin, flute, trumpet, voice, singer, band, orchestra, concert, audience, lyrics, melody, rhythm…
Expressions with OUT: eat out (cenar fuera), go out (salir), find out (descubrir), work out (ejercitarse / resolver), hang out (pasar el rato), figure out (comprender), check out (echar un vistazo)

📘 Unit 6 — The environment · Verbs to talk about energy

Medio ambiente: recycle (reciclar), climate change (cambio climático), carbon footprint (huella de carbono), pollution (contaminación), renewable energy (energía renovable), solar power (energía solar), wind power (energía eólica), endangered species (especies en peligro), global warming (calentamiento global), waste (residuos/desperdiciar), save (ahorrar), protect (proteger)
Verbos de energía: save energy, waste energy, use, produce, generate, consume, reduce, turn off / on

📘 Unit 7 — Arranging a party · Expressions with ABOUT

Organizar una fiesta: arrange (organizar), decorate (decorar), invite (invitar), bring (traer), prepare (preparar), guest (invitado), host (anfitrión), celebration (celebración)
Expressions with ABOUT: worry about (preocuparse por), think about (pensar en), talk about (hablar sobre), be excited about (estar emocionado por), be crazy about (estar loco por), be nervous about (estar nervioso por), care about (importarle a uno)

📘 Unit 8 — Directions and movement · Science

Direcciones y movimiento: go straight ahead (todo recto), turn left/right (girar izquierda/derecha), go across (cruzar), go along (ir por), go past (pasar de largo), on the corner (en la esquina), next to (al lado de), opposite (enfrente de), between (entre), at the traffic lights (en el semáforo)
Ciencia: biologist (biólogo/a), astronomer (astrónomo/a), chemist (químico/a), physicist (físico/a), scientist (científico/a), experiment (experimento), laboratory (laboratorio)

📘 Unit 9 — Jobs · Work as/in/for · Expressions with IN · Work vs Job

Profesiones: doctor, nurse, teacher, engineer, lawyer, journalist, firefighter, policeman/woman, chef, waiter/waitress, accountant, architect, vet, pilot, flight attendant…
Work as / in / for: work as a doctor (trabajar como), work in an office / a hospital (trabajar en un lugar), work for a company (trabajar para una empresa)
Expressions with IN: be interested in (estar interesado en), be good at (ser bueno en) — nota: "at" no "in", specialise in (especializarse en), believe in (creer en)
Work vs Job: work (trabajo como concepto, incontable) · job (trabajo como puesto, contable: a job / jobs)

📘 Unit 10 — Time linkers · Collocations

Conectores temporales: when (cuando), as soon as (tan pronto como), until / till (hasta que), while (mientras), before (antes de), after (después de), by the time (para cuando), whenever (siempre que), as long as (mientras que)
Collocations temporales: have lunch/dinner, do homework, go to bed, wake up, get up, start school, finish work, arrive home

📘 Unit 11 — Fun · Verbs with object + infinitive · Expressions with MAKE

Diversión y ocio: have fun (divertirse), enjoy oneself (disfrutar), play games, go out with friends, watch a film, listen to music, spend time, relax…
Verbs with object + infinitive: want someone to do (querer que alguien haga), ask someone to do (pedir a alguien que haga), tell someone to do (decir a alguien que haga), expect someone to do (esperar que alguien haga), would like someone to do (gustaría que alguien hiciera)
Expressions with MAKE: make plans (hacer planes), make a decision (tomar una decisión), make a phone call (hacer una llamada), make a mistake (cometer un error), make someone laugh (hacer reír a alguien), make noise (hacer ruido), make the bed (hacer la cama), make friends (hacer amigos), make sure (asegurarse)
