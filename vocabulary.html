📚 Topics — Vocabulario por temas

Vocabulario organizado por unidades temáticas con ejercicios interactivos.

📚 Topics

🏫 School ✈️ Travel 🍔 Food 👤 Personality 🎬 Films & TV 🎵 Music 🌍 Environment 🎉 Events 🔬 Science 💼 Jobs 🤒 Health 🎉 Leisure

🏫 School — El colegio ⬆ Go Up

📖 Subjects (asignaturas): maths, science, history, geography, English, Spanish, art, music, PE (physical education), IT (information technology)
✏️ Objects (objetos): pen, pencil, ruler, rubber, sharpener, book, notebook, bag, desk, board, computer, calculator, dictionary
👩‍🏫 Verbs (verbos): study, learn, teach, explain, understand, listen, write, read, draw, calculate, revise, pass (an exam), fail (an exam)
🏢 Places (lugares): classroom, library, canteen, gym, playground, laboratory, office, sports field

✈️ Travel — Viajes ⬆ Go Up

🚗 Transport: car, bus, train, plane, bike, taxi, ferry, underground / metro, tram, coach, motorbike
🏨 Accommodation: hotel, hostel, campsite, B&B, apartment, guesthouse, resort
🔁 Travel verbs: book, reserve, pack, arrive, depart, catch (a train), miss (a flight), check in, explore, visit, travel, go on holiday
🧳 Travel vocab: ticket, passport, luggage, suitcase, map, guide, journey, trip, flight, boarding pass, destination, souvenir
📍 Directions: turn left/right, go straight on, cross the road, at the corner, opposite, next to, between

🍔 Food & Drink — Comida y bebida ⬆ Go Up

🍽️ Meals (comidas): breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack, dessert, brunch
🥦 Food groups: fruit (apple, banana, orange), vegetables (carrot, tomato, onion), meat (chicken, beef, pork), fish, dairy (milk, cheese, yoghurt), grains (bread, rice, pasta)
👨‍🍳 Cooking verbs: cook, boil, fry, bake, roast, grill, chop, mix, peel, pour, add, stir, cut
🍝 Restaurant: menu, waiter / waitress, order, bill, tip, main course, starter, dessert, take away, book a table
🥤 Drinks: water, juice, milk, coffee, tea, soda, smoothie, hot chocolate

👤 Personality — Cómo somos (U1) ⬆ Go Up

🔵 Adjectives: serious, cheerful, brave, positive, talented, creative, active, laid‑back, charming
🔗 Collocations: make a mistake / an appearance · do your best / work · get a present / a reputation · lose a race / your life · take a risk / a photograph · form a group / an opinion
💬 JUST phrases: It was just a joke / Tom's just arrived / The book was just fantastic

🎬 Films & TV — Cine y televisión (U3) ⬆ Go Up

🎥 Types of films: documentary, comedy, action film, animated film, horror, thriller, science fiction (sci‑fi), romantic comedy (rom‑com)
📺 TV programmes: game show, chat show, sports programme, reality show, sitcom, cartoon, soap (opera), drama series, talent show
🔗 GET expressions: get angry, get bored, get a drink, get better, get there

🎵 Music & Instruments — Música (U5) ⬆ Go Up

🎸 Instruments: trumpet, violin, guitar, piano, drums, saxophone, keyboard, bass guitar
🎤 Making music: download a song, write songs & lyrics, enter a talent show, go on tour, start a band, record / release a single, enter the charts, win a competition, play a gig
🔗 Phrasal verbs with OUT: start out, find out, run out of, come out, go out, sort out

🌍 Environment — Medio ambiente (U6) ⬆ Go Up

♻️ The environment: litter, water / air pollution, flood, recycling, melt, extinct, rubbish, waste, laws, smog
⚡ Energy verbs: recycle, throw away, waste, charge, reuse, leave on standby, disconnect, save

🎉 Events & Parties — Eventos (U7) ⬆ Go Up

⏰ Future time: the day after tomorrow, the (week/month) after next, before too long, next (week/month/year), from now, in the near future
🎈 Arranging a party: choose a theme, decorate the room, draw up a guest list, get permission, book/hire a DJ, send out invitations, organise food & drinks
💬 ABOUT phrases: about (10 o'clock), about to (go home), What about you?, sorry about (that), think about (it)

🔬 Science & Movement — Ciencia (U8) ⬆ Go Up

🧭 Direction & movement: away from, around, down, backwards, towards, up and down, forwards, up
🔬 Science: laboratory, scientist, discovery, experiment, machine, invention, cure, invent, do research

💼 Jobs & Work — Trabajo (U9) ⬆ Go Up

💼 Jobs (pay & conditions): work long hours, earn money, get paid holidays, give in your notice, on‑the‑job training, start a career, a challenging job, be promoted, be successful
⏰ Time expressions WITH IN: in the end, in the next few years, in the past, in a day, in three hours, in June
🔗 Work as / in / for: work as a teacher, work in television, work for a big bank
⚖️ Work vs Job: a good job · difficult work

🤒 Health & Illness — Salud (U10) ⬆ Go Up

⏱️ Time linkers: when, then, until
🤒 Illness collocations: take exercise, get better, see a doctor, have an operation, make an appointment, feel sick

🎉 Leisure & Free Time — Ocio (U11) ⬆ Go Up

🎮 Fun & leisure: have fun, enjoy oneself, play games, go out with friends, watch a film, listen to music, spend time, relax
🔗 Verbs + object + infinitive: want someone to do, ask someone to do, tell someone to do, expect someone to do, would like someone to do
🔗 Expressions with MAKE: make plans, make a decision, make a phone call, make a mistake, make someone laugh, make noise, make the bed, make friends, make sure
