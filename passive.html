Passive Voice

Transforma frases activas en pasivas.

📖 Teoría rápida

Fórmula general: to be + past participle
Active: Someone stole my bike. → Passive: My bike was stolen.

Present Simple: is / are + p.p. → English is spoken worldwide.
Past Simple: was / were + p.p. → The church was built in 1800.
Present Perfect: has / have been + p.p. → A new hospital has been built.
Future: will be + p.p. → The results will be announced soon.
Modals: can / must / should be + p.p. → This must be signed.
Present Continuous: is / are being + p.p. → The house is being painted.
Past Perfect: had been + p.p. → The room had been cleaned.
going to: is / are going to be + p.p. → A new shop is going to be opened.
🔁 Doble objeto (give, send, show, tell...)
They gave me a book. → I was given a book. o A book was given to me.
They sent her a letter. → She was sent a letter.
Active: Someone built this house → Passive: This house was built.
Active: They gave her a prize → Passive: She was given a prize.
