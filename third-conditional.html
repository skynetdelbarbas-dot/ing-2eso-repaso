🔀 Third Conditional

Aprende a hablar de situaciones hipotéticas en el pasado — cosas que no sucedieron pero que podrían haber ocurrido (nivel B2).

📖 Teoría rápida — Third Conditional

🔧 Estructura

If + Past Perfect , would have + Past Participle

If I had studied, I would have passed the exam.
(No estudié → no aprobé. Es una situación imposible en el pasado.)

🎯 ¿Cuándo lo usamos?

Para expresar una situación imaginaria en el pasado:
— La condición (if clause) es imposible porque ya pasó.
— El resultado (main clause) es lo que habría pasado si la condición se hubiera cumplido.

If she had woken up earlier, she wouldn't have missed the bus.

🔄 Tercera vs Segunda Condicional

2nd Conditional → situación improbable en el presente/futuro:
If I had more money, I would buy a car. 🚗 (ahora no tengo, pero podría en el futuro)

3rd Conditional → situación imposible en el pasado (ya pasó):
If I had had more money, I would have bought a car. 🚗 (no tenía dinero entonces → no lo compré)

📌 Contracciones

If I'd studied = If I had studied
I would've passed = I would have passed
I wouldn't have failed = I would not have failed
I'd've gone (informal) = I would have gone
If he had known the truth, he wouldn't have been so angry. 😤
She would have passed if she had studied harder. 📖
If we had left earlier, we would have caught the train. 🚄
